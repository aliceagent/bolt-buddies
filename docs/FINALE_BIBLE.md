# Bolt Buddies — FINALE Creative Bible (source of truth)

North star: *the players think the game ends when they win — it actually ends
when they turn around.* Non-violent; reunion over punishment; two skills
(Light-Beam + Time-Freeze) only; softlock-proof; every beat auto-advances.

## Climax beat sheet
PHASE 2 "THE RUNAWAY TANTRUM": the 3rd core is unplugged but the tantrum keeps
spinning. KOBI's OFF switch — a crescent-moon SLEEP button — is on his BACK
behind his tail-fan; he has no arms and can't reach it. The fight inverts into
rescuing him.
- 2.0 THE TURN (core 3 touched): eye keeps spinning, wall-clamps pop, eye drifts
  free like a balloon; camera snaps to low wide hero shot.
- 2.1 RECONFIGURE: center ramp rises; two light-perches lower; eye does slow
  telegraphed figure-8 drift (contact = soft bounce, never damage); SLEEP button
  on the back revealed; Bolt (caged, ringside) barks & points.
- 2.2 CO-OP LOOP (role-agnostic): BEAM mesmerizes the eye (moth) → its back turns
  to the ramp; FREEZE the tail-fan → blades become stairs; climb & HOLD the SLEEP
  button; beam-held + button-held = 3s co-op hold; progress = a giant eyelid
  slowly closing; GLARE fizzles into confetti. Progress floors at 50%, never
  resets; unlimited retries.
- 2.3 SCRIPTED WOBBLE (once, at ~50%): KOBI breaks free, faces the exit door,
  scared — "if I sleep, who watches the door?" Loop reopens; 2nd hold always
  completes; music drops to a music-box; slit pupil dilates round (becomes a face).
- 2.4 POWER-DOWN (~45s auto): the click ("boop"); ring-lights shut off outward-in;
  eye descends & counts down wrong; shell unfolds to reveal a teapot-sized real
  KOBI (scale-reversal shot); Bolt's cage fail-safe pops → zoomies/nuzzle;
  exit lights gold; buddies+Bolt walk toward it but camera HOLDS on tiny KOBI
  alone; Bolt stops in the doorway, looks back (the dog decides); they TURN BACK;
  KOBI refuses rescue one line then "carry me anyway"; rides out on Bolt's back.
